About The Event

AfriConnect Summit 2023

The AfriConnect Summit is a premier gathering of innovators, entrepreneurs, and leaders from across Africa. This year's theme is "Digital Transformation: Unlocking Africa's Potential."

Join us in Lagos, Nigeria from December 5-7, 2023 for three days of inspiring keynotes, hands-on workshops, and unparalleled networking opportunities.

This event will feature speakers from top African tech companies, investors looking to fund the next big idea, and policymakers shaping the future of technology across the continent.
