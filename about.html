About The Event

AfroFusion Summit 2023

The AfroFusion Summit is a celebration of African innovation, culture, and creativity. This year's theme is "Rooted in Heritage, Growing the Future."

Join us in Accra, Ghana from November 15-17, 2023 for three days of inspiring keynotes, hands-on workshops, cultural performances, and unparalleled networking opportunities.

This event will feature speakers from top African tech companies, investors looking to fund the next big idea, and cultural icons showcasing the richness of African heritage.
